## How to get an earlier slot than 2026-07-01

Ranked by likelihood of working:

1. **App refresh (DLT Smart Queue)** — cancellations free up slots daily. Check 2-3×/day, especially 08:00-09:00 and 18:00-20:00 ICT. Often catches a slot 1-2 weeks out.
2. **DLT-certified driving school** (Honda Driving School, Toyota Driving School, BSR, etc.) — runs theory + practical + video + physicals on-site. Issues a certificate → at DLT only the license-printing step (~30 min, no exam queue). Cost ~6 000-8 000 THB, 15-20 hours over 2-3 days. **Fastest path if budget allows.**
3. **Different DLT branch** — try smaller offices in same province. Bangkok area: Bang Khae, Taling Chan, Min Buri, Nonthaburi, Samut Prakan often shorter than Chatuchak. Residence Certificate generally accepted at any DLT in the province it was issued for.
4. **Walk-in at small offices** — some give limited walk-in tickets at 06:30-07:00. Big Bangkok offices basically don't. Office-dependent.
5. **Touts / brokers** outside DLT (2 000-5 000 THB "fast-track") — gray area, often resell publicly available cancellation slots. Not recommended.
6. **Phone booking** — DLT does not accept phone bookings or rescheduling. Won't work.

Strategy: keep 2026-07-01 as fallback, parallel-pursue (1) + price out (2).

### Notes specific to Pran Buri office

- Small provincial office → walk-in 06:30-07:00 has better odds than at Bangkok DLTs.
- 1 July as the earliest slot suggests the office may accept foreigners only on specific weekdays (common at small offices). Find out which day(s) and target walk-in for that morning.
- Nearby alternatives to check in DLT Smart Queue: **DLT Prachuap Khiri Khan** (provincial main), **DLT Hua Hin** (if listed). Sometimes one of them has slots weeks earlier.
- DLT-certified driving schools: unlikely in Pran Buri itself; **Hua Hin** has options. Worth a call for next-week availability.

## Day-of-exam flow at DLT

1. Document check at counter
2. Physical tests: color (red/green/yellow), depth perception (sticks), peripheral vision, brake reaction time
3. Safety video (~1 hour) — ask for English version
4. Theory test on computer — 50 questions, need ≥45. English & Russian languages available. Practice via app **DLT QR LICENCE** or PDF "Thai Driver License Test" (online).
5. Practical test on DLT range (bring own car/motorbike or rent on-site at some offices): parallel parking, hill stop, cone slalom, line driving
6. Pay fee → photo → license printed same day (2-year temporary)

After 2 years: renew without exam → 5-year license.
