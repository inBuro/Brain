# Sends Follower — Demo VoiceOver script

**Summary**: Final VoiceOver narration for the Sends Follower demo video. Canonical, locked copy in the Fadercraft voice (Steinkamp; same "Stay in the music." sign-off as the Control XL demo). Source of truth for product-page and Gumroad phrasing — see [[gumroad-description-sends-follower]].

**Last updated**: 2026-06-26

---

Sends Follower turns send levels into modulation sources.

Track follows the send amount of a single track.

Choose Send A, B, C, or D. Or switch to Manual for mouse or MIDI control.

Return follows the activity of an entire return bus.

Peak mode follows the highest send.

Total mode follows the combined activity of every track sending to that bus.

Instead of adding more effect, you're shaping its behavior.

One source can control up to 8 parameters, each with its own range and polarity.

Fadercraft Sends Follower.

Stay in the music.
