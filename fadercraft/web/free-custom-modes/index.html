How to import

  1. Open Novation Components and connect your Launch Control XL MK3 via USB.
  2. Pick the device, switch to Custom Modes.
  3. Select the target slot, click Upload Custom Mode, choose the matching N.syx file. Repeat for each mode you want.
  4. Click Send to Device on each slot.
  5. Press a side button on the controller to switch modes.

Every mode keeps UNDO on button 8 (Cmd-Z) and REDO on button 9 (Cmd-Y).
