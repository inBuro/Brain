Sends Follower turns send levels into modulation sources.
Instead of increasing the send level, you're shaping the effect's behavior.
One source can control up to 8 parameters, each with its own range and polarity.
Track follows the send amount of a single track.
Choose Send A, B, C, or D. Or switch to Manual for mouse or MIDI control.
Return follows the activity of an entire return bus.
Peak mode follows the highest send.
Total mode follows the combined activity of every track sending to that bus.
