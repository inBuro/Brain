Sends Follower turns track sends and return buses into modulation sources.
Sends Follower includes two devices.
Track — follows the send amount of a single track.
One source can control up to eight parameters, each with its own range and polarity.
Return — follows the activity of an entire return bus.
In Peak mode, it follows the highest send value.
In Total mode, it follows the combined send amount from all tracks.
